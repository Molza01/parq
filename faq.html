Frequently Asked Questions

What is PARQ?
PARQ is a platform that helps users connect, ask, and explore knowledge efficiently.
How can I create an account?
Click on Sign Up and fill in your details to create a new account.
Is PARQ free to use?
Yes, the basic version of PARQ is completely free to use.
Can I use PARQ on mobile?
Yes, PARQ is fully responsive and works on all devices.
How do I reset my password?
Use the "Forgot password?" link on the login page to reset your password.
Is my data secure on PARQ?
We use the latest encryption technologies to keep your data safe.
Can I delete my account?
Yes, contact our support team to request account deletion.
Does PARQ support multiple languages?
Currently, PARQ supports English. More languages are coming soon!
How do I ask a question?
After logging in, use the "Ask Me" button to start your query.
Who can I contact for help?
Reach out to support@parq.com for any assistance.
